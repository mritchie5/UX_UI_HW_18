Hello, I'm Maya

Skills

As a UX/UI designer, I possess a diverse set of skills that enable me to create intuitive and user-centered digital experiences. My skills include user research, wireframing, prototyping, information architecture, and interaction design. I also have a strong understanding of design principles, typography, and color theory, which allows me to craft visually appealing interfaces.

Tools

In my design process, I leverage a variety of industry-standard tools to bring ideas to life. I am proficient in using Figma, a design software used to create wireframes and high-fidelity mockups to build interactive prototypes that help validate and communicate design concepts effectively.

Overview

I am a passionate UX/UI designer with a strong commitment to creating exceptional digital experiences. My approach is user-centric, and I believe in understanding the needs and pain points of users to design solutions that are both functional and aesthetically pleasing.

By collaborating closely with cross-functional teams, I ensure seamless integration of design into the development process. My goal is to deliver products that not only meet business objectives but also delight users and leave a lasting positive impression
